REVENUE PROJECTIONS (ReduceImages-style)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📈 TRAFFIC TARGETS:
- Month 1-3: 1,000 monthly users
- Month 4-6: 10,000 monthly users  
- Month 7-12: 100,000+ monthly users
- Year 2: 1M+ monthly users (ReduceImages scale)

💰 REVENUE STREAMS:
1. **Premium Subscriptions (60% of revenue)**
   - Target: 2-5% conversion rate
   - Average: $5/month per subscriber
   - 100K users × 3% conversion = 3,000 subs = $15K/month

2. **Google AdSense (40% of revenue)**
   - Free users generate ad revenue
   - Target: $2-5 RPM (revenue per thousand pageviews)
   - 100K users × 3 images/month × $3 RPM = $900/month

**Total Projected Revenue at Scale: $15K-20K/month**
