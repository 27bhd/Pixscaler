About Pixscaler

Pixscaler was built to solve a common problem: the need for fast, reliable image resizing without compromising quality. As a full-stack developer with experience in building scalable web applications, I understand the importance of having the right tools for the job.

This tool combines modern web technologies with efficient image processing to deliver professional-grade resizing that works seamlessly across all devices. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pixscaler really free to use?

Yes, Pixscaler is completely free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or limitations. You can resize unlimited images without creating an account.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, your images never leave your device. Pixscaler processes all images directly in your browser using HTML5 Canvas technology. This ensures complete privacy and faster processing.

What image formats does Pixscaler support?

Pixscaler supports JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and HEIC/HEIF formats for input. You can export resized images as JPEG, PNG, or WebP.

How many images can I resize at once?

You can resize over 100 images at once with Pixscaler's bulk processing feature. All processed images are packaged into a convenient ZIP file for easy download.

What is the maximum file size supported?

Pixscaler supports images up to 50MB each. The maximum output dimension is 8000x8000 pixels.

Can I use Pixscaler for commercial projects?

Absolutely! Pixscaler is free for both personal and commercial use. There are no restrictions on how you use the resized images.
